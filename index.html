Who We Are

Welcome to St. Margaret’s Metal Recycling where sustainability meets innovation in metal recycling. We are fully dedicated to responsibly repurposing metal resources, reducing our environmental impact and contributing to a greener future.

Sustainability Commitment

St. Margaret’s aims to become a zero-emissions facility by 2027. That is why we recently installed solar panels on site which offer significant environmental benefits, such as reduced air and water pollution. They also provide a sustainable energy resource while simultaneously reducing our carbon footprint.
